Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. WellnessIQ is designed for health-conscious individuals and families, and we take our responsibility to protect your data seriously.

1. Who We Are

WellnessIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered fitness coaching application available on iOS and Android. Our app provides personalized fitness coaching, workout plans, and health guidance for users of all experience levels interested in achieving specific fitness goals.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: support@wellnessiq-app.info

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use WellnessIQ:

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address (when creating an account)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Fitness goal selection (weight loss, cardio, strength, stamina)
  • Experience level and training preferences
  • Questions and messages submitted to the AI coaching feature

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Subscription status and tier
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address and general location (country/region level only)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalize our AI coaching services to your fitness goals
  • Process your subscription and manage your account
  • Send important service-related communications (receipts, policy updates)
  • Improve our AI coaching quality and app performance
  • Monitor for misuse and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for targeted advertising. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. AI Coaching Conversations

When you submit questions to our AI coaching feature, your messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI API to generate responses. Please be aware:

  • Conversations are processed by Anthropic in accordance with their privacy policy
  • We store conversation history to provide context for follow-up questions
  • We may review anonymized conversation samples to improve coaching quality
  • Do not submit sensitive personal information (financial data, passwords, medical records) through the coaching chat

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

WellnessIQ is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at support@wellnessiq-app.info and we will delete that information.

For users between 13 and 18, we encourage parental involvement in account setup and ongoing use of the application.

6. Third-Party Services

WellnessIQ uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication and user account management
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management and billing
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — App distribution and payment processing
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI coaching response generation

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We use HTTPS for all data transmission between the app and our servers. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services.

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@wellnessiq-app.info. We will respond within 30 days.

You can delete your account directly within the app settings or by emailing us. Account deletion will permanently remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at support@wellnessiq-app.info.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of WellnessIQ after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

Email: support@wellnessiq-app.info
Website: www.wellnessiq-app.info
